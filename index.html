THE MAN, THE MYTH, THE MEME

Who is PewDiePie you ask? His real name is Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, he is a a Swedish YouTube creator living in Brighton, UK. His Youtube career began on 29 th April 2010, and since 2013, PewDiePie’s YouTube channel has been the most subscribed channel on the platform with over 94 million subscribers.

He started his YouTube journey with a gaming channel following the Let’s Play formula. He gained popularity playing horror games, especially Amnesia: The Dark Descent. Soon after, his content started featuring vlogs, comedy skits and much wider variety of gaming videos. In 2013, PewDiePie became the most subscribed YouTube channel at 12 million subscribers – a number that doubled in next year alone. His content kept evolving and his style changed, so did his sense of humor. In 2016, PewDiePie became the first YouTube channel to reach 50 million subscribers. Nowadays, PewDiePie has over 90 million subscribers and is on the way to reach 100 million subscribers. In his content he focuses primarily on comedy and commentary – he discusses memes, books, movies and recent news and, of course, video games.

But one thing has not changed at all – our beloved Felix. He is still the same passionate guy recording videos from his room and talking directly to the camera. He makes us laugh, he motivates us and shares his everyday stories with us. And for that we love him!
